# Hyderabad Waste Collection Route Optimization (nVisionX’26 – GHMC)

Smart, data-driven routing model for optimizing solid waste collection from Garbage Vulnerable Points (GVPs) in Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation (GHMC).

## 1. Vehicle Allocation

<img width="732" height="409" alt="Ve All Sum" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/41a17250-68e9-4d92-8b4a-4ab08e5bed9e" />


- Capacity-based allocation by cluster waste volume.  
- Minimum 60% vehicle utilization constraint with backup vehicles for peak loads.  

---

## 2. Route Optimization

<img width="746" height="385" alt="Rou " src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/885696b9-3c5a-4901-9f33-16e5674df7ef" />


- Nearest-neighbor algorithm with capacity constraints.  
- Cluster-wise route construction to minimize distance while meeting waste demand.  

---

## 3. Scheduling Efficiency Metrics


<img width="732" height="409" alt="SC EF Me" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/98ae65eb-ba1b-41c4-bdcb-2d1315dde950" />


- Time-based scheduling within working hours, including service and travel times.  
- Breaks between trips and summary KPIs for trips, hours, and utilization.
